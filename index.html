The lives of our women and families are better.

We care about our community. We provide for our kids and their families in order to enrich lives, and we serve the entire community by empowering others. With Future Children International, children excel. Youth thrive and Families succeed.

CHILDREN

FCI leads by empowering children. We advocate for awareness on important issues through our events and programs. Our programs provide opportunities for the community to learn, grow and reinforce the leadership and well-being of our kids. FCI support and empowers children to reach their goals and celebrate their achievements. When children comes together, amazing things happen.

YOUTH

At FCI, kids build healthy friendships and develop confidence. FCI succeeds in providing active environments designed for kids’ learning and growth. FCI Youth Development programs engage kids’ minds and bodies, helping them develop the confidence to pursue their interests. Check out our wide variety of programs, including afterschool programs, STEAM education, camps, sports leagues and more.

CHILDCARE

Our childcare services are designed to help children learn. We offer special opportunities for children to develop personal and social values, to exercise reason and to develop the ability to make decisions. We engage children through personalized care and activities. Through our open door policy, parents and grandparents are welcome to visit anytime to participate in their child’s learning experience.
