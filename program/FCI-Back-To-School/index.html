Project Description

INFORMATION

For more information or questions regarding a sponsorship, please contact Alhaji Mohamed Kanu +23277150549 .

FCI Back to Bash is a yearly event which brings together all Future Children International Kids! Join nearly 30 children in Sierra Leone for Success and a brighter new school year and help them get a brighter future in their lives. The energy in the Confrence room is incredible, fueled by the stories of all the children who attend this program and our keynote speaker and President Sierra Leone branch Mr. Alhaji Mohamed Kanu who deliver a wonderfull message to all the kids.

EVENT DETAILS

1st Back to School Bash in Sierra Leone
Sunday, September 13, 2020
Ubuntu Confrence Center
10:30am-12:00pm

KEYNOTE SPEAKER

Kindness Champion Mr Alhaji Mohamed Kanu will inspire you to live a more meaningful and productive life, showing why kindness really does matter. His captivating authenticity will challenge you to change the way you live and think. Because the life you transform with kindness is your own. And kindness IS contagious.

Through he inspiring stories and touching authenticity, Mr Kanu reveals ways to push through the “tough” by shifting our focus onto kindness. he will leave you feeling uplifted, refreshed and equipped with tools for setting your trials aside and enjoying everyday life.
