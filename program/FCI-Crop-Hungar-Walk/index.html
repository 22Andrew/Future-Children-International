An integral part of this Huger Walk on the Run celebrates and reinforces important lessons while showing World Leaders to support deprived vonnurable community more especially in Africa. The 5K walk serves as an achievement for those participating and gives them a tangible sense of healthy goal setting and success. both young and old take part in all season to complete a 5K (3.1-mile) race. The 5K is a celebration and an opportunity for each child to literally cross the finish line!
