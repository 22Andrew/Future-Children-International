The opportunities are endless

Future Children International is a bold leader, empowering children. Our childre’s programs provide opportunities for the community to learn, grow and reinforce the leadership and well-being of children. Our programs raise awareness on a variety of children’s issues, provide leadership and involvement opportunities and actively promote gender equity and social justice issues. We believe deeply in workinng with children where they are in their life and strive to walk that path with them.

Financial Assistance

Future Children International financial assistance provides for the needs of our children's, who cannot afford the full cost of their tution fees and programs. FCI is committed to being accessible to all, and works hard to support those who may be in challenging financial situations.
